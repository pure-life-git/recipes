# Chocolate Peppermint Shortbread

## Ingredients

- 1/2 c butter, room temp
- 1/3 c brown sugar (75g)
- 3/4 tsp salt
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 1 c flour (120g)
- 1/3 c cocoa powder (30g)
- 2 egg whites
- 6 tbsp sugar (85g)
- 1/2 tsp peppermint extract

## Procedure

1. In a large bowl, blanch the brown sugar and butter. Mix in the salt, egg yolk, and vanilla extract until combined. Add the flour and cocoa powder and mix until just combined.
2. Press the dough into a prepared 8"x8" pan into about 1/4" layer. Dock with a fork and bake at 350F until set and dry, 30-35 minutes.
3. While the cookies bake, in a large mixing bowl, combine the egg whites, sugar, and a pinch of salt. Set over a double boiler and whisk constantly until the mixture reaches 175F.
4. Remove the mixture from heat and whip in a stand mixer until light and glossy. Whip in the peppermint extract.
5. Allow the shortbread to cool to room temp. Spread the meringue over the shortbread and bake at 225F until the meringue is dry and crisp, about 2 hours. Allow to cool inside the oven.
