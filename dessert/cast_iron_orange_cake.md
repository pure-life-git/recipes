# Cast Iron Orange Cake

## Ingredients

- 2 c sugar (400g)
- 2 navel oranges
- 2 tsp vanilla extract (9g)
- 1/4 tsp fennel seeds, lightly toasted, ground
- 1/2 tsp salt (1g)
- 1 c butter, room temp (226g)
- 2 egg yolks, room temp
- 2 c flour (240g)
- 1/4 c semolina flour (50g)
- 2 tsp baking powder (8g)

## Procedure

1. Preheat the oven to 350F with a cast iron pan on the middle rack.
2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the sugar, zest of one orange, vanilla extract, ground toasted fennel seeds, and salt, and rub together with your fingers to release the orange oils.
3. Cut oranges into 8 pieces each and place in a food processor, pulsing until a puree consistency is reached. Reserve 1 1/2 cups and set the rest aside.
4. Add the butter into the sugar mixture and, using a stand mixer, whip until the mixture is light and fluffy. Add the egg yolks and mix until fully incorporated.
5. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, semolina flour, and baking powder. Into the stand mixer, add the 1 1/2 cups of orange puree, starting on slow speed and ramping up to medium to incorporate everything. Do the same with the dry ingredients.
6. Brush the skillet with olive oil and pour the batter in, ensuring it's spread evenly. Bake at 350F for 35-45 minutes, until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Let cool at least 40 minutes before serving.
