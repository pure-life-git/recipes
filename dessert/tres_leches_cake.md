# Tres Leches Cake

## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 c flour (180g)
- 1 tbsp baking powder
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 6 large eggs, separated
- 3/4 + 1 tbsp c granulated sugar (150g)
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 1 1/2 c whole milk, divided (1/2 c + 1 c)
- 12 oz evaporated milk
- 1/2 c sweetened condensed milk
- 1 1/2 c heavy cream, divided (1/2 c + 1 c)
- 1 tsp ground cinnamon, divided (1/2 tsp + 1/2 tsp)

## Procedure

1. In a large bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, and salt.
2. In a medium bowl, beat the egg white to soft peaks. In a separate bowl, black the egg yolks with 3/4 cup sugar and vanilla extract.
3. Add the egg yolk mixture to the flour mixture and stir to combine. Add in 1/2 cup whole milk and mix until there are no lumps. Slowly fold in the egg whites in 3 additions.
4. Pour the batter into a prepared 9"x13" pan and bake at 350F until a toothpick comes out clean, about 30-35 minutes.
5. While the cake is baking, in a large bowl, combine evaporated milk, 1 cup whole milk, sweetened condensed milk, 1/2 cup heavy cream, and 1/2 tsp cinnamon. Set aside.
6. Once the cake is cooled, use a fork to poke holes all over the top of the cake. Pour the milk mixture over the cake, going in batches so that the cake soaks up the entire mixture.
7. Whip 1 cup heavy cream and 1 tbsp sugar until medium peaks. Spread over the top of the cake and flatten. Sprinkle with cinnamon, cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate until ready to serve.
