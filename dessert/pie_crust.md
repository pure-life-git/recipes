# Pie Crust

## Yield: Two Crusts

## Ingredients

- 3 3/4 c flour (440g)
- 2 tbsp sugar
- 1 tsp salt
- 20 tbsp butter, cubed, frozen
- 1 c ice water (227g)

## Procedure

1. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, and salt.
2. Add in the pieces of butter and coat in the flour mixture. Quickly break apart the butter with your fingers until it resembles the size of peas
3. Make a well in the center of the mixture and add 1/2 cup of ice water. Work together with a fork, adding extra water just until the dough barely comes together
4. Turn out onto a work surface and chop up the dough, which should still be shaggy, with a bench scraper. Work it together into a pile and then continue to chop and toss it.
5. Using your hands, squish the dough into a square and pat it to about 3/4" thickness. Cut it in half, stack, and roll out to a rectangle. Cut in half and stack again, wrap in plastic wrap and chill in the fridge at least 2 hours.
