# Claire Saffitz Pumpkin Pie

## Ingredients

- 1 pie crust
- 3 tbsp butter
- 1/3 c honey (112g)
- 3/4 c heavy cream, room temp (170g)
- 4 large eggs, room temp
- 1/4 c brown sugar (50g)
- 15 oz pumpkin puree
- 2 tsp ground cinnamon
- 1 1/2 tsp ground ginger
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp ground allspice
- 1/2 tsp grated nutmeg
- 1/4 tsp ground cloves

## Procedure

1. Roll out pie crust and drape into a pie dish. Fold over and crimp the edges and dock the bottom with a fork. Place in the freezer until frozen. Line the inside of the crust with foil and fill to the top with pie weights or beans
2. Bake in a 425F oven for 20 minutes, take out the pie weights and foil, and bake at 325F for an additional 20-25 minutes, until the crust is golden brown.
3. In a small saucepan, brown the butter. Once golden brown, whisk in the honey and bring to a boil over medium heat.
4. Remove from heat and slowly whisk in heavy cream. Set aside.
5. In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs and brown sugar, mixing until lightened in color. Add in the pumpkin puree, cinnamon, ginger, vanilla extract, salt, allspice, nutmeg, and cloves, and whisk until fully combined.
6. Slowly stream in the cream mixture, mixing until homogenous.
7. Pour the filling into the pie crust and bake at 325F for 45-60 minutes, until the sides are set and the middle is a little bit wobbly. Let the pie cool completely in the oven (turned off) to prevent cracking.
