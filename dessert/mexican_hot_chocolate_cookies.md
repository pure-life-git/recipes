# Mexican Hot Chocolate Cookies

## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 c flour (180g)
- 1/2 c cocoa powder (42g)
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp ground cayenne
- 3 tsp ground cinnamon, divided (2 tsp + 1 tsp)
- 1/2 c butter, room temp (113g)
- 1 1/2 c brown sugar (320g)
- 1 egg, room temp
- 2 tsp vanilla extract
- Mini marshmallows, frozen
- 1/4 c sugar (50g)

## Procedure

1. In a medium bowl, whisk flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, salt, ground cayenne, and 2 tsp ground cinnamon.
2. In the bowl of a stand mixer, beat butter and brown sugar on medium until light and fluffy. Add in the egg and vanilla extract and continue to beat until creamy. Mix in the flour mixture on low speed and mix until just combined. Portion into 1 oz balls and chill for at least 2 hours, up to overnight.
3. Preheat oven to 350F. In a small bowl, mix together 1 tsp cinnamon and 1/4 c sugar. Flatten a portion of dough and place 5 frozen marshmallows in the middle, folding the edges in to envelope the marshmallows. Roll into a ball and coat in cinnamon sugar. Place on a lined baking sheet about 3 inches apart.
4. Bake for 10-12 minutes, rotating halfway, until puffy and bit of marshmallow peek through the surface. Let cool completely.
