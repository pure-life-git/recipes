# Lemon Polenta Cookies

## Ingredients

- 4 tsp fresh rosemary, finely chopped
- 1 1/2 tbsp lemon juice
- Zest of 4 lemons
- 1 1/2 c flour (180g)
- 1/2 c polenta (82g)
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/2 cream of tartar
- 1 c butter, room temp (226g)
- 1 1/2 c sugar (300g)
- 1 large egg

## Procedure

1. Preheat oven to 350F. Combine the rosemary and lemon juice in a small bowl and set aside.
2. In a medium bowl, combine the flour, polenta, baking soda, salt, and cream of tartar. Set aside.
3. In the bowl of a stand mixer, beat the butter and sugar on medium speed until light and fluffy.
4. Add in the egg, lemon zest, and rosemary mixture and mix to combine on low speed. Add the flour mixture in two additions, mixing until just combined.
5. Portion the dough into 12 even pieces and chill until firm. Roll in sugar and place about 3 inches apart on a lined baking sheet.
6. Bake for about 8 minutes, before lifting and dropping the baking sheet on both sides and rotating. Bake for another 6-8 minutes, until the edges are golden brown.
