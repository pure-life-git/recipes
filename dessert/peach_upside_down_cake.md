# Peach Upside Down Cake

## Ingredients

- 1/2 c butter, softened
- 3 large peaches, ripe, 1/2" slices
- 1 1/4 c sugar (250g)
- 1 c flour (120g)
- 3/4 tsp baking powder
- 1/4 tsp ground nutmeg
- 3 large eggs

## Procedure

1. Preheat the oven to 350F. Grease a 9" cake pan or pie plate and line the bottom with parchment paper.
2. Line the bottom of the pan with the peach slices.
3. In a saucepan, combine 1/2 c (100g) sugar and 1/4 c water. Set over medium-high heat until the mixture turns amber and the sugar is dissolved. Remove from heat immediately and pour over the peaches at the bottom of the pan.
4. In a medium bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, and ground nutmeg.
5. In a separate medium bowl, beat together the butter and 3/4 c (150g) sugar until light and fluffy. Beat in the eggs 1 at a time.
6. Mix in the flour mixture, stirring until just combined. Spread the batter evenly over the peaches and caramel.
7. Bake for 30-35 minutes, until the top is golden brown and cake is set. Remove from the oven and let cool slightly.
8. Run a knife around the edges of the pan, place a platter on top, and invert the cake onto the platter. Serve warm.
