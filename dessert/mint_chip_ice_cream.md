# Mint Chip Ice Cream

## Ingredients

- 1 c whole milk
- 3/4 c sugar
- 2 c mint leaves
- 2 c heavy cream
- 4 egg yolks
- Chocolate chips, chunks, or flakes

## Procedure

1. In a pot, combine milk, sugar, heavy cream, and a pinch of salt. Cook over medium heat until you see steam rising from the mixture.
2. Stir in the mint leaves. Take off the heat and cover, allowing to cool to room temperature. Then refrigerate overnight.
3. Strain the cream mixture into a pot and reheat over medium low. Separately, whisk together the egg yolks in a bowl.
4. Once the cream mixture has steam coming off of it, slowly stream 2 cups of the cream mixture into the egg yolks, whisking constantly. Return this mixture back to the pot and continue to cook, whisking constantly, until it reaches 180F.
5. Set the pot over an ice bath and whisk until cool. Pour through a sieve into the pot of an ice cream maker. Churn based on the machine instructions.
6. Once the ice cream has been churned, add in chocolate pieces and then freeze until firm.
