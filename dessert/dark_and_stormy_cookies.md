# Dark and Stormy Cookies

## Ingredients

- 1 c crystallized ginger, finely chopped
- 1/4 c dark rum
- 3 c flour (360g)
- 2 tsp baking soda
- 2 tsp ground ginger
- 1 tsp ground cinnamon
- 1/2 tsp ground cloves
- 1/2 tsp ground cayenne
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1 c brown sugar (213g)
- 1 c sugar, divided (1/2 c + 1/2 c) (200g)
- 1/2 c butter, room temp, cut into tbsp (113g)
- 1/3 c molasses (113g)
- 2 eggs
- 2 c powdered sugar (226g)
- 2 tbsp dark rum
- 2 tbsp lime juice
- 2 limes
- Salt

## Procedure

1. Preheat the oven to 350F. In a small bowl, combine the chopped ginger and 1/4 c dark rum and cover. Microwave for 90 seconds, or until the rum starts to boil. Stir, cover, and set aside.
2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, ground ginger, cinnamon, ground cloves, ground cayenne, and salt. Set aside.
3. In the bowl of a stand mixer, add the brown sugar, sugar, butter, and molasses and mix at medium speed until light in color.
4. Add the eggs to the stand mixer one at a time, mixing to incorporate. Add in the dry ingredients and mix until just combined.
5. Strain liquid from chopped ginger and add in the ginger to the mixer. Mix on low until evenly distributed.
6. Portion the dough into 2 tbsp balls, roll into sugar, and place on a lined baking sheet, gently pressing to flatten. Leave 1 inch between cookies.
7. Bake for exactly 12 minutes and let cool on the tray for 5 minutes before transferring to a cooling rack to cool completely.
8. In a medium bowl, combine the powdered sugar, 2 tbsp dark rum, lime juice, and a pinch of salt. Dip each cooled cookie in the glaze and place on a cooling rack set in a baking sheet. Zest a lime over the cookies and allow the glaze to fully dry.
