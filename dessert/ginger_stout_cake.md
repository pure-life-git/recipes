# Ginger Stout Cake

## Ingredients

- 2 tbsp butter, room temp (28g)
- 1/2 c demerara sugar (125g)
- 1 c stout
- 1 c molasses (340g)
- 1/2 tsp baking soda (3g)
- 2 c flour (240g)
- 1 tbsp ground ginger
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- 1/4 tsp ground cloves
- 1/4 tsp grated nutmeg
- 1/4 tsp pepper
- 1/4 tsp allspice
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 3 tbsp ginger, grated
- 3 large eggs, room temp
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 1 c brown sugar (213g)
- 1 c sugar (200g)
- 3/4 safflower oil

## Procedure

1. Preheat oven to 350F. Grease a Bundt pan well with butter and dust with demerara sugar, dumping out any excess.
2. In a medium saucepan over medium heat, bring the stout and molasses to a simmer. Remove from the heat and whisk in the baking soda. Let cool to room temperature.
3. In a large bowl, sift together the flour, ground ginger, cinnamon, ground cloves, grated nutmeg, pepper, allspice, and salt. Set aside.
4. In the bowl of a stand mixer, whisk together the fresh ginger, vanilla extract, brown sugar, and sugar on medium speed for 5 minutes.
5. With the mixer on low, add the oil and mix for another 5 minutes. Slowly add in the stout mixture and continue mixing for 5 minutes.
6. Add in the dry ingredients in two additions, mixing until just combined.
7. Pour the batter into the sugar coated pan and bake for 55-65 minutes, until a cake tester comes out clean. Let cool for 15 minutes and then release from pan before allowing to cool completely.
