# Buttercream

## Ingredients

- 2 c butter, room temp (452g)
- 1 tsp vanilla extract (5g)
- Pinch of salt
- 4 c powdered sugar (452g)
- 3 tbsp cream (43g)

## Procedure

1. In the bowl of a stand mixer, whisk the butter on medium-high speed until light and fluffy.
2. Whip in the vanilla extract and salt. Gradually add in the powdered sugar in 3 additions, alternating with 1 tbsp of cream.
3. Continue to whip on medium-high until smooth and fluffy.
