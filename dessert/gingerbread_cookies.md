# Gingerbread Cookies

## Ingredients

- 14 tbsp butter, cut into pieces (198g)
- 3 tbsp fresh ginger, freshly grated
- 1 tbsp ground ginger
- 2 tsp ground cinnamon
- 1 tsp pepper
- 1/2 tsp grated nutmeg
- 2 c powdered sugar (226g)
- 3 tbsp molasses (64g)
- 1 tbsp vanilla extract (14g)
- 1 1/4 tsp salt
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1 large egg
- 2 c flour (240g)
- 1/4 c sugar (50g)
- 2 tsp ground ginger

## Procedure

1. In a medium saucepan over medium heat, brown the butter. Pour into the bowl of a stand mixer and whisk in fresh ginger, ground cinnamon, pepper, and grated nutmeg. Set aside until totally cool, about 20 minutes in the fridge or 2 hours at room temp.
2. Add powdered sugar, molasses, vanilla, salt, and baking soda to the butter. Mix on low until creamy and combined.
3. Add in the egg and mix on medium until pale and fluffy. Add the flour and mix until just evenly combined. Cover and chill at least 2 hours or up to 3 days.
4. In a small bowl, stir together the sugar and 2 tsp ground ginger. Preheat the oven to 375F.
5. Portion the dough into 30g balls, about 2 tbsp. Roll in the ginger sugar and arrange on a lined sheet tray at least 1 inch apart. Bake until set and lightly browned around the edges, about 10 minutes or 11-13 minutes if baking from frozen.
