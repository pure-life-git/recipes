# Lemon-Turmeric Cookies

## Ingredients

- 3 tbsp olive oil
- 1/2 tsp ground turmeric
- 3/4 c sugar (150g)
- 2 tbsp lemon zest
- 4 oz cream cheese, room temp
- 1 large egg, room temp
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 3/4 tsp baking soda
- 1 1/2 c flour (180g)
- 3/4 c powdered sugar (90g)

## Procedure

1. In a large bowl, whisk together the olive oil and turmeric until dissolved.
2. Add in the sugar, lemon zest, cream cheese, egg, and vanilla extract and whisk until combined.
3. Mix in the baking soda, salt, and flour just until combined. Cover and refrigerate until scoopable consistency.
4. Scoop into 1 1/2 tbsp balls and roll in powdered sugar. Place on a lined baking sheet and bake at 350F until crinkled on top and no longer wet looking, 15-17 minutes.
