# Lemon Poppy Seed Bars

## Ingredients

- 1 c butter, room temp (226g)
- 2/3 c sugar (133g)
- 3/4 tsp salt
- Zest of 2 lemons
- 2 tsp vanilla extract
- 2 1/4 c flour (270g)
- 2 tbsp poppy seeds
- 1 c lemon curd

## Procedure

1. Preheat oven to 350F. Prepare an 8 inch baking pan.
2. Combine sugar, salt, and lemon zest, massaging with your fingers until fragrant. Add the butter and beat on medium speed until light and fluffy. Mix in the vanilla extract.
3. With the mixer on low speed, add in the flour and mix until just combined. Add in the poppy seeds and continue to mix until just incorporated. The dough should be slightly crumbly.
4. Set aside 1 cup of the crumbly dough and transfer the rest into the prepared pan and flatten along the bottom. Spread the curd in an even layer and top with the reserved crumbly dough.
5. Bake until the crumb topping is golden brown, rotating every 20 minutes, for 40-48 minutes. Cool completely before removing from pan and cutting into 16 squares.
