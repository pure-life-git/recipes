# Crystallized Meyer Lemon Bundt Cake

## Ingredients

- 3 c flour (360g)
- 2 1/2 tsp baking powder (10g)
- 1 tsp salt (3g)
- 1/2 tsp baking soda
- 1 c milk, room temp (206g)
- 2 tsp vanilla extract
- 3/4 c Meyer lemon juice, divided (1/2 c + 1/4 c)
- 1 tbsp Meyer lemon zest
- 1 3/4 c + 2/3 c sugar (350g + 133g)
- 4 large eggs, room temp
- 1 1/3 c + 2 tbsp olive oil

## Procedure

1. Preheat oven to 350F. Grease a bundt pan and dust it with flour. Set aside.
2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, salt, and baking soda.
3. In a 2 cup measuring cup, whisk together milk, vanilla extract, and 1/4 c lemon juice.
4. In a large mixing bowl, combine 1 3/4 c sugar and lemon zest, massaging together with your fingers until fragrant.
5. Add in the eggs and mix on medium speed until the mixture is thick. With the mixer still running, slowly stream in 1 1/3 c olive oil and continue to mix until fully emulsified.
6. With the mixer on low, alternate adding in the flour and milk mixtures, adding about 1/3 at a time. Mix until just combined.
7. Pour the batter into the prepared pan and bake at 350F until a skewer comes out clean in the middle, 45-55 minutes.
8. While the cake is cooling, whisk together 1/2 c lemon juice, 2/3 c sugar, and 2 tbsp olive oil until the sugar dissolves.
9. While the cake is still warm, poke holes in the bottom and pour glaze over, letting it soak into the cake. Remove the cake from the pan and set on a wire rack set into a baking sheet. Pour the rest of the glaze over the top of the cake, using a brush to catch any drips. Let cool completely before serving.
