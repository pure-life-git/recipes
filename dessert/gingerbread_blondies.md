# Gingerbread Blondies

## Ingredients

- 1 c butter
- 1 1/2 c brown sugar (300g)
- 2 tbsp molasses
- 1 tbsp fresh ginger, finely grated
- 2 large eggs
- 2 tbsp vanilla extract
- 2 c flour (240g)
- 2 tsp baking powder
- 2 tsp ground ginger
- 1 tsp ground cinnamon
- 3/4 tsp salt
- 1/4 tsp ground allspice
- 1/4 tsp black pepper
- 1/8 tsp ground cloves

## Procedure

1. In a small saucepan, brown the butter. Transfer to large bowl and whisk in the brown sugar and molasses. Set aside to cool.
2. Add in the ginger, eggs, and vanilla extract, mixing to combine. Whisk in the salt and baking powder.
3. Mix in the flour, baking powder, ground ginger, ground cinnamon, salt, ground allspice, black pepper, and ground cloves.
4. Pour batter into a parchment lined and buttered 9"x9" pan. Bake at 350F for 25-40 minutes, until mostly done in the middle.
