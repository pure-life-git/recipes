# Claire Saffitz Apple Pie

## Ingredients

- 1 pie crust

### For the filling

- 3 lb granny smith apples, 1/2" wedges
- 1/2 c brown sugar (100g)
- 3 tbsp cornstarch
- 2 tsp ground cinnamon
- 2 tsp apple cider vinegar
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 2 tbsp butter, small cubes, chilled

### For the topping

- 1 c flour (120g)
- 1 c rolled oats (113g)
- 1/3 c brown sugar (65g)
- 1 tsp ground cinnamon
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 10 tbsp butter, small cubes, chilled

## Procedure

1. To make the filling, combine all the ingredients in a large bowl and mix, ensuring all apple pieces are evenly coated. Set aside.
2. To make the topping, combine all the ingredients in a medium bowl and mix with your hands, breaking apart the butter pieces until the mixture has reached a sandy consistency.
3. Roll out your pie crust and drape into a pie plate. Crimp the edges and place in the freezer until ready for assembly
4. Line the bottom of the dough with apple slices, and then dump the rest in on top. Place little chunks of chilled butter in between the apple slices and then top with the crumble, pressing it into the filling.
5. Place on a baking sheet and bake at 400F for 20 minutes before reducing the heat to 350F and baking for an additional 60-90 minutes, until you see juices bubbling.
