# Ina Garten's Brownie Pudding
## Ingredients
- 1 c butter, melted
- 4 large eggs, room temp
- 2 c sugar (400g)
- 3/4 c cocoa powder
- 1/2 c flour (60g)
- 1 tbsp vanilla extract, or 1 bean vanilla seeds
- 1 tbsp framboise liqueur, optional
## Procedure
1. Preheat the oven to 325F. Butter a 2 quart baking dish.
2. In a mixing bowl, beat the eggs and sugar on medium high speed until light and fluffy.
3. In a separate bowl, sift together the cocoa powder and flour.
4. Reduce the mixer to low and add the vanilla, framboise liqueur (if using), and the flour mixture. Slowly pour in the butter and mix until just combined.
5. Pour the batter into the baking dish and place in a large baking pan. Add boiling water to the larger pan until it comes up halfway to the smaller baking dish. 
6. Bake for exactly 1 hour. Let cool completely before serving.
