# Mascarpone

## Ingredients

- 2 1/2 c heavy cream (20 fl oz)
- 2 1/2 tbsp lemon juice

## Procedure

1. In a medium saucepan, bring the heavy cream to a simmer over medium heat. Whisk in the lemon juice and continue to simmer, whisking occasionally, until the mixture has thickened and coats the back of a spoon.
2. Transfer to an ice bath and allow to fully cool. Once cooled, pour into a cheesecloth lined sieve set over a bowl. Cover and store in the fridge for 24 hours.
3. Squeeze all the liquid out of the mascarpone and store in an airtight container for up to 4 days.
