# Cinnamon Rolls with Cream Cheese Frosting

## Ingredients

- 2 1/4 c whole milk, divided (3/4 c + 1 1/2 c)
- 5 3/4 c flour, divided (30g + 660g)
- 1 large egg
- 1 envelope instant yeast
- 1/3 c granulated sugar (70g)
- 1 tbsp salt
- 1/2 c butter, room temp
- 1/2 c butter
- 1 c brown sugar
- 2 tbsp ground cinnamon
- 2 tbsp heavy cream
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 4 tbsp butter, room temp
- 8 oz cream cheese, room temp
- 1 1/4 c powdered sugar (150g)
- 1 tsp lemon zest
- 1/4 tsp salt

## Procedure

1. Whisk 3/4 cup whole milk and 30g flour in a medium saucepan until smooth. Place over medium heat and cook until thickened, whisking constantly. Scrape mixture into a large mixing bowl and let cool.
2. Add 1 large egg, instant yeast, 1/3 cup sugar, 1 tbsp salt, and remaining 1 1/2 cups milk into the mixing bowl and whisk to combine. Add the remaining 660g flour.
3. Mix on low speed until combined. Increase to medium-low and mix until the dough is smooth and elastic, 18-24 minutes. It should pass the windowpane test.
4. Add 1/2 cup softened butter cut into pieces into the dough and mix until incorporated. Continue to mix at medium speed until the dough pulls away from the sides of the bowl, 5-7 minutes.
5. Remove the bowl from the stand mixer, cover with a damp towel, and let sit in a warm spot until doubled in volume, about an hour.
6. While the dough is rising, brown 1/2 cup butter and transfer to a bowl to cool slightly. Add in brown sugar, ground cinnamon, heavy cream, and 1/2 tsp salt and mix to combine.
7. Butter two 9" cake pans. Transfer the dough to a lightly floured work surface and roll into a roughly 24"x13" rectangle. Spread the filling evenly over the dough.
8. Roll the dough, starting with a short side. Cut into 12 equally sized rounds. Nestle 6 rounds in each cake pan, cover with a damp towel, and let rest in a warm spot until doubled in size, a little over a half hour.
9. Bake in a 350F oven until golden on top and puffed, 25-30 minutes.
10. While the rolls are baking, beat together 4 tbsp room temp butter and cream cheese. Add 1 1/4 c powdered sugar, 1 tbsp heavy cream, 1 tsp lemon zest, and 1/4 tsp salt and beat until smooth. Adjust texture with powdered sugar or cream accordingly, the consistency should be thick but spreadable.
11. Allow the rolls to cool in the pan for 5 minutes before icing them.
