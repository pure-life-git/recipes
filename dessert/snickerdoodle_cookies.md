# Snickerdoodles

## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 c flour (180g)
- 1 tsp cream of tartar
- 1/2 tsp baking soda
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 10 tbsp butter, room temp (141g)
- 3/4 c sugar (150g)
- 1 egg
- 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
- 1 tbsp ground cinnamon

## Procedure

1. Preheat the oven to 375F. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, cream of tartar, baking soda, and salt.
2. In the bowl of a stand mixer, beat together the butter and sugar until fluffy. Beat in the egg and vanilla extract. Add in the flour mixture and mix until just combined.
3. In a small bowl, combine 2 tbsp of sugar and 1 tbsp ground cinnamon. Portion the dough into 20g balls, about a heaping tbsp, roll in sugar, and place on a lined baking sheet, at least 3 inches apart.
4. Bake the cookies until just set in the center, 10-12 minutes.
