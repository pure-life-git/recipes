# Ladyfinger

## Ingredients

- 3 eggs, separated
- 1/2 c granulated sugar, divided (50g + 50g)
- 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 1 c flour (120g)
- 5 tsp cornstarch
- 1/4 c powdered sugar, for dusting

## Procedure

1. In a large bowl, combine egg yolks, 50g sugar, salt, and vanilla extract. Whisk vigorously until lightened in color and fluffy.
2. In a separate large, clean mixing bowl, begin to whisk the egg whites on low speed. Once frothy, slowly stream in the remaining 50g of sugar and increase the speed to medium-high. Beat to soft peaks.
3. Fold the beaten egg whites into the yolk mixture in 3 additions, taking care not to deflate the whites.
4. In a separate bowl, combine the flour and cornstarch. Sift the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients, folding together. Transfer to a piping bag with a large round tip.
5. Onto a lined baking sheet, pipe 4 inch long ladyfingers. Dust liberally with powdered sugar and bake in a 350F oven until golden brown, 12-15 minutes.
