# Brown Butter & Maple Pumpkin Pie

## Ingredients

- 1 pie crust
- 6 tbsp butter
- 15 oz pumpkin puree
- 1/2 c maple syrup (160g)
- 3/4 c brown sugar (150g)
- 3 large eggs
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1 tsp ground cinnamon
- 1/2 tsp ground ginger
- 1/4 tsp ground nutmeg
- 1/4 tsp ground cloves
- 1/4 tsp ground allspice
- 3/4 c heavy cream (180g)

## Procedure

1. Roll out the pie crust to about a 12in circle, then drape over a 9in pie plate and fold over + crimp the edges. Then chill in the fridge for ~30 minutes, or the freezer for ~15 minutes while the oven preheats to 375F
2. Dock the bottom of the crust, then line the inside with parchment paper/foil and fill with pie weights. Bake at 375F for 20 minutes, remove the weights, then bake for an additional 15–20 minutes, until the crust is golden brown
3. In a medium saucepan, brown your butter. Transfer to a heat safe bowl in an ice bath to help the butter cool.
4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the pumpkin puree, maple syrup, brown sugar, eggs, vanilla, salt, and spices. Add the cooled butter and heavy cream and whisk to combine.
5. Pour the filling into the par-baked crust and bake at 325F for 50–60 minutes, until the edges are set and the middle has a little wobble.
6. Once the pie is done, turn the oven oﬀ and prop the door open to allow it to cool without cracking.
7. To make the whipped cream, combine the heavy cream, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract in a stand mixer and whip until soft peaks. Top the pie with whipped cream and freshly grated nutmeg.
