# Vietnamese Coffee Swirl Brownies

## Ingredients

- 1 c butter
- 8 oz unsweetened chocolate, coarsely chopped
- 1 tsp instant espresso powder
- 3/4 tsp salt
- 2 c sugar
- 2 tbsp cocoa powder
- 4 eggs, room temp
- 1 tbsp vanilla extract
- 1 c flour
- 2/3 c cream cheese, softened
- 3 tbsp sweetened condensed milk
- 1 1/2 tsp ground espresso
- Flaky sea salt

## Procedure

1. Preheat oven to 350F. Prepare a 9 inch baking pan.
2. In a large saucepan over medium heat, combine the butter, chocolate, instant espresso powder, and salt. Stir occasionally until the chocolate and butter have melter.
3. Whisk in the sugar and cocoa powder until smooth, then add in the eggs and vanilla extract. Whisk in the flour just until no streaks reamin.
4. Pour the mixture into the prepared pan and spread evenly.
5. In a separate bowl, beat together the cream cheese and condensed milk until smooth. Mix in the ground espresso until just combined, you should still be able to see specks.
6. Dollop the cream cheese mixture on top of the brownie batter and swirl, creating a marbled effect. Sprinkle with flaky sea salt.
7. Bake until the top is set, 30-40 minutes. A toothpick in the center should come out slightly gooey. To get clean pieces, let cool completely, chill for an hour, invert, and cut into bars upside down.
