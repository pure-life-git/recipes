# Ginger Cheesecake Cookies

## Ingredients

- 6 oz cream cheese, chilled
- 1/2 c powdered sugar (57g)
- 1 egg yolk
- 2 tsp vanilla extract (9g)
- 2 1/2 c flour (300g)
- 1 tbsp ground ginger
- 2 tsp ground cinnamon
- 1 tsp baking soda (6g)
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 3/4 tsp salt (2g)
- 10 tbsp butter, softened (141g)
- 1 c sugar (200g)
- 1 egg
- 1/2 c crystallized ginger, finely chopped (62g)
- 1/3 c molasses (113g)
- 2 tbsp fresh ginger, finely chopped

## Procedure

1. In a medium bowl, beat together the cream cheese, powdered sugar, egg yolk, and vanilla extract. Transfer to a piece of plastic wrap, roll into a log, and freeze until firm.
2. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, ground ginger, ground cinnamon, baking soda, black pepper, and salt.
3. In a medium bowl, cream the butter and sugar together. Beat in the egg, crystallized ginger, molasses, and fresh ginger.
4. Slowly add the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients, mixing on low speed until just combined.
5. Portion the dough into 18 pieces, about 45g each. Flatten each ball in your hand and place a slice of the cream cheese filling in the middle. Roll into a ball, cover in sugar, and place on a lined baking sheet.
6. Bake in a 350F oven until the center is just set, 15-17 minutes.
