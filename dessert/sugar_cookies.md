# Sugar Cookies

## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 c butter, room temp
- 1 1/4 c sugar (250g)
- 1 large egg
- 1 large egg yolk
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 3 c flour (360g)
- 1 1/4 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp baking powder

## Procedure

1. In a large bowl, cream the butter and sugar. Mix in the egg, egg yolk, and vanilla extract.
2. Add in the flour, salt, and baking powder and mix until just combined.
3. Shape the dough into a disc, wrap in plastic, and refrigerate 2 hours - 4 days.
4. Roll the dough out to 1/4" and cut into shapes. Bake at 325F until the edges are just golden brown, 12-15 minutes.
