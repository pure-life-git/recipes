# Choccy Chip Cookies

## Ingredients

- 1/2 c butter
- 1 c flour (120g)
- 1/2 tsp baking soda
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/4 c brown sugar (50g)
- 1 large egg, room temp
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 1 c chocolate chips, or to preference

## Procedure

1. Brown the butter. Once golden brown, transfer to a heatproof bowl, along with two ice cubes, and allow to cool.
2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and salt.
3. Add the brown sugar into the butter and whisk until combined. Whisk in the egg and vanilla extract.
4. Mix the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients, stopping just once everything is incorporated. Let sit for 30 minutes.
5. Form the dough into balls measuring roughly 2 tbsp. Place on a lined baking sheet and freeze.
6. Bake in a 375F oven until the edges are crisp, 9-12 minutes.
