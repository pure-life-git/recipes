# Claire Saffitz Tiramisu

## Ingredients

- 3/4 c strong coffee, espresso preferred
- 1/4 c kahlua
- 3 large egg yolks
- 1/4 c sugar (50g)
- 2 tsp instant coffee
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1 c mascarpone, chilled (8.5 oz)
- 1/2 c heavy cream, chilled (4 oz)
- 24 ladyfingers
- 1/2 c cocoa powder, for finishing

## Procedure

1. In a shallow bowl or a plate with a lip, mix the coffee and kahlua.
2. In a small saucepan, combine sugar, instant coffee, vanilla, salt, and 1/4 cup water and bring to a boil.
3. In a large mixing bowl, add in the egg yolks and beat on medium speed. While the mixture is going, slowly stream in the instant coffee mixture and continue to beat until thick and room temp.
4. In a large bowl, beat the mascarpone and heavy cream on medium-high until stiff peaks form. Add in the egg yolk mixture and continue to beat until stiff peaks form again.
5. To assemble, soak a ladyfinger in the kahlua mixture and lay in the bottom of a baking dish. Coat the bottom of the disk in ladyfingers and then cover with the whipped cream mixture.
6. Repeat this layering until all the filling has been used. You should end with a layer of filling on top.
7. Dust with cocoa powder, shaking off the excess. Cover with plastic and refrigerate at least 12 hours before serving.
