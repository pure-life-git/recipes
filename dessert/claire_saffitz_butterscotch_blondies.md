# Claire Saffitz Butterscotch Blondies

## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 + 2/3 c brown sugar (300g + 140g)
- 19 tbsp butter
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tbsp + 2 tsp vanilla extract
- 1 2/3 c flour (225g)
- 3/4 tsp baking soda
- 2 large eggs, cold
- Flaky salt, for finishing

## Procedure

1. Into a small saucepan, add 2/3 cups brown sugar, 7 tbsp butter, 1/2 tsp salt, 2 tsp vanilla extract, and 2 tbsp water. Bring to a boil over medium-high heat. Reduce to medium-low and cook until mixture reaches 260F.
2. Pour the mixture out onto a lined sheet pan and allow to cool in the fridge or freezer until brittle.
3. In a small saucepan, brown the remaining 12 tbsp of butter. Transfer to a heatproof bowl and add 2 ice cubes and 1 1/2 cups brown sugar, whisking to combine.
4. Whisk in the eggs one at a time. Add in the vanilla extract. In a separate bowl, combine flour, baking soda, and 1/2 tsp salt.
5. Combine the wet and dry ingredients and let rest for 5 minutes.
6. Break apart the butterscotch and mix about 2/3 into the batter. Spread the batter into a prepared 8"x8" pan and scatter the remaining butterscotch on top. Bake at 350F for 35-50 minutes, until the edges are golden brown and firm in the middle.
