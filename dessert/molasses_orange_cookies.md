# Molasses Orange Cookies

## Ingredients

- 1 c sugar, divided (135g + 65g)
- 3 tsp orange zest, divided (2 tsp + 1 tsp)
- 2 1/4 c flour (270g)
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
- 1 1/2 tsp ground ginger
- 1/2 tsp ground cloves
- 1/4 tsp ground allspice
- 1/4 tsp ground black pepper
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 12 tbsp butter, room temp
- 1/3 c brown sugar (75g)
- 1 large egg yolk
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 1/2 c molasses

## Procedure

1. In a small bowl, combine 135g sugar and 2 tsp orange zest. Use your fingers to rub the sugar and zest together.
2. In a separate bowl, whisk together flour, baking soda, cinnamon, ginger, cloves, allspice, pepper, and salt.
3. In a large mixing bowl, cream the butter, brown sugar, 65g sugar, and 1 tsp orange zest. Add in the egg yolk and vanilla extract, mixing until combined.
4. Mix in the molasses. Then add the dry ingredients and mix until just combined.
5. Form dough into roughly tablespoon balls and roll in orange sugar. Set 2" apart on a lined baking sheet and bake at 375F until the edges have set, about 11 minutes.
