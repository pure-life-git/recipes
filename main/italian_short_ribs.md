# Italian Short Ribs

## Ingredients

- 3 tbsp flour
- 6 lb beef short ribs, english cut
- 1/4 c olive oil
- 2 oz pancetta, chopped
- 2 yellow onions, roughly chopped
- 6 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tsp red pepper flakes
- 2 carrots, chopped
- 2 tbsp tomato paste
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 1 c red wine
- 14 oz diced plum tomatoes
- 1 c beef broth
- 1/4 c balsamic vinegar
- 2 bay leaves
- 2 sprigs fresh rosemary
- 2 sprigs fresh thyme
- 1 tbsp dried oregano

## Procedure

1. On a plate, mix flour with 1 tsp salt and 1/2 tsp pepper. Coat the short ribs in the seasoned flour, shaking off the excess.
2. In a large dutch oven, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the short ribs in one at a time, searing on all sides, and removing once done.
3. Add the pancetta and cook until slightly crispy. Then add the onions and cook till translucent. Add the garlic and red pepper flakes, cooking until fragrant, before adding in the carrots, tomato paste, and sugar, mixing to combine.
4. Pour in the red wine, scraping the bottom of the pan as you do so. Bring the mixture to a boil and stir in the tomatoes, beef broth, and balsamic vinegar. Bring back to a boil.
5. Bundle the bay leaves, rosemary, and thyme with some twine before adding into the mixture along with the oregano and the seared short ribs.
6. Cover with a lid and place in a 350F oven for 2 hours, or until fork tender.
7. Alternatively, place in a slow cooker for 3 hours on high, or 6 hours on low, again until fork tender.
8. Top with gremolata before serving.
