# French Onion Pasta

## Ingredients

- 1/4 c olive oil
- 3 yellow onions, thinkly sliced
- 6 garlic cloves, grated
- 8 oz cremini mushrooms, sliced
- 1 tbsp fresh thyme
- 1/2 c white wine
- 2 tbsp flour
- 2 c beef broth
- 2 c water
- 12 oz orechiette
- 1 tbsp sherry

## Procedure

1. In a large pot, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add in the onions, along with a large pinch of salt. Cook until deeply caramelized, the consistency should be jammy.
2. Add the garlic, mushrooms, and thyme to the pot and cook until the mushrooms begin to soften. Pour in the white wine, scraping the bottom of the pot as you do so. Simmer off about 1/2 the wine.
3. Add in the flour, beef stock, and water. Bring to a boil and add in the pasta. Cook the pasta to al dente per the box's instructions.
4. Take off heat and stir in the sherry.
