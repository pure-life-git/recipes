# Pasta Bake

## Ingredients

- 1 lb cascatelle pasta
- 1 lb ground beef, 85%
- 26 oz pasta sauce
- 1 onion, diced
- 2 c milk (412g)
- 3 tbsp butter (42g)
- 3 tbsp flour (23g)
- 1 c parmesan, finely grated
- 8 oz fresh mozzarella, sliced

## Procedure

1. In a medium pot, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned. Season with salt and pepper.
2. Add in the onion and cook until softened. Pour in the pasta sauce and about a cup of water, along with a parmesan rind if you have it. Let simmer for 15-20 minutes.
3. While the sauce simmers, cook the pasta until just under al dente.
4. Add the cooked pasta into the sauce and mix.
5. In a separate small saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat and whisk in the flour.
6. Slowly whisk in the milk and continue to whisk until thickened.
7. Take off the heat and add in 1/2 cup parmesan, whisking to combine.
8. Add the pasta into a baking dish, pour bechamel over the top, and add a layer of fresh mozzarella on top, along with the rest of the parmesan.
9. Cover with foil and bake at 350F for 15-20 minutes. Remove the foil and broil until the cheese on top is bubbly and golden. Let sit 10 minutes before serving.
