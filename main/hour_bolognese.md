# Hour Bolognese

## Ingredients

- 50g dried mushrooms
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 clove garlic, smashed
- 1 small carrot, diced
- 1 stalk celery, diced
- 1/2 onion, diced
- 50g prosciutto, diced
- 200g ground pork
- 3/4 c red wine
- 1 3/4 c passata (400ml)
- 1/4 tsp grated nutmeg

## Procedure

1. Submerge the dried mushrooms in lukewarm water for 30 minutes. Drain and set aside.
2. In a large pot over medium heat, warm the olive oil. Add in the garlic and cook until slightly browned. Discard garlic.
3. Add in the carrot, celery, onion, and prosciutto and cook until vegetables are soft and the prosciutto has browned. Add in the ground pork and mushrooms and cook until pork has browned, stirring constantly.
4. Pour in the red wine, making sure to scrape the bottom of the pot as you do so. Simmer until the wine volume has decreased by half. Add in the passata and nutmeg, stirring to combine.
5. Cover the mixture with a lid and continue to simmer for an hour, adding water or chicken stock as necessary so that it doesn't dry out.
