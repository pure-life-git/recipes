# Barbacoa Shredded Beef

## Ingredients

- 3 lb chuck roast, cut into 6-8 chunks
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp pepper
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil
- 1/2 c beef broth
- 1/4 c apple cider vinegar
- 1/4 c lime juice, 1-2 limes
- 4 chipotle peppers in adobo, chopped
- 6 cloves garlic, chopped
- 1 tbsp ground cumin
- 1 tbsp dried oregano
- 1/4 tsp ground cloves
- 3 large bay leaves

## Procedure

1. Pat the roast dry and season with salt and pepper on all sides. In a dutch oven, heat the vegatable oil over medium heat. Sear the roast on all sides and remove.
2. Add the beef broth, apple cider vinegar, lime juice, chipotle peppers, minced garlic, cumin, oregano, and cloves to the pot. Stir together and bring to a boil.
3. Add the chuck roast back into the pot.
4. If cooking in the dutch oven, cover with a lid and place in a 325F oven until the beef is fork tender, 8-10 hours.
5. If cooking in a slow cooker, transfer liquid and beef to the slow cooker and cook on low until the beef in fork tender, 8-10 hours.
6. Once the beef is done, shred with two forks and let marinate in the liquid for at least 10 minutes before serving.
