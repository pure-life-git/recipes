# Cowboy Butter Steak Pasta

## Ingredients

- 2 ribeye steaks
- 1 tsp paprika
- Salt
- Pepper
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tbsp butter
- 6 tbsp butter
- 1 shallot, diced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced or grated
- 2 tsp paprika
- 1 tsp Dijon mustard
- 1 tsp chili flakes
- 1 tsp thyme
- 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
- 1/4 c cream
- 3 tbsp lemon juice
- Zest of one lemon
- 2 tbsp parsley, chopped
- 1 tbsp chives, chopped
- 1 lb rigatoni, cooked al dente
- 1/4 c parmesan, grated
- Salt
- Pepper

## Procedure

1. Season the steaks liberally on both sides with salt, pepper, and paprika. Let sit at room temp for 15-20 minutes.
2. Heat a large cast iron skillet over medium-high heat. Add in 1 tbsp olive oil. Pat steaks dry and cook for 1 1/2 minutes per side, until a golden crust forms. Add in 1 tbsp butter and baste for about 20 seconds. Remove and rest under foil.
3. Reduce the heat to medium-low and add in 6 tbsp butter. Let it brown slightly before stirring in the shallot, garlic, paprika, Dijon mustard, chili flakes, thyme, and cayenne pepper. Stir to combine.
4. Pour in the cream and 1 c pasta water, then bring to a simmer and let thicken.
5. Add in the lemon juice, lemon zest, parsley, and chives. Toss the pasta into the pan, along with the parmesan, and continue to simmer until thick. Season with salt and pepper. Slice the steak thinly and serve over sauced pasta.
