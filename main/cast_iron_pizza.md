# Cast Iron Pizza

## Ingredients

- 300g bread flour
- 6g salt
- 6g instant yeast
- 210g warm water
- 10g olive oil
- Pizza sauce
- Low moisture, part skim mozzarella

## Procedure

1. In a large bowl, whisk together the bread flour, salt, and instant yeast. Pour in the water and olive oil.
2. Using your hands, mix the wet and dry ingredients together until a cohesive dough forms. Turn out onto a lightly floured work surface and knead until the dough has a smooth, elastic feel to it. Stretch into a ball, place in a lightly oiled bowl, cover, and let rest in a warm place for 30 minutes.
3. Once the 30 minutes are up, turn the dough out onto a lightly floured work surface and stretch and fold it 5-10 times. Stretch it back into a ball and place back into a lightly oiled bowl for another 30 minutes. Repeat this a total of 3 times.
4. After the last set of stretch and folds, let rest, covered, for 90 minutes. While this final proof is happening, grease a cast iron skillet with olive oil.
5. Turn the dough out of the bowl and work into a disc. Place the dough into the greased cast iron skillet and work into the edges, ensuring the dough is in an even layer along the bottom of the pan. Cover and let rest a final time for 90 minutes. This is a great time to make your pizza sauce if you haven't already.
6. Drizzle some olive oil over the top of the dough and poke with your fingers to make little divots (think focaccia). Par bake in a 500F oven for 15 minutes.
7. Take the crust out, smother with sauce, cheese, and any desired toppings, and place back into the oven for another 15 minutes, or until the toppings are done cooking.
