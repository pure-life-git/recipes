# Cauliflower Tacos

## Ingredients

- 1 head cauliflower, cut into bite sized pieces
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 1/2 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp chili powder
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- 1/2 tsp salt

## Procedure

1. Preheat oven to 400F. Add cauliflower onto a baking sheet, drizzle with olive oil, and coat in spices.
2. Roast for 20-25 minutes, until brown around the edges.
