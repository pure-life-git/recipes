# Carne Asada

## Ingredients

- 2 jalapenos
- 1 medium tomato, cored, quartered
- 1 yellow onion, quartered
- 5 cloves garlic, peeled
- 2 tbsp sugar
- 1/4 tsp ancho chile powder
- 1 tbsp black pepper
- 1 tbsp salt
- 1/2 bunch cilantro, rinsed
- 1/3 c orange juice
- 3 tbsp lime juice
- 1/4 c mirin
- 12 oz lager beer
- 2 lb skirt steak
- 2 tbsp olive oil

## Procedure

1. Under a broiler, roast jalapenos until skin begins to blacken. Pull the steams and the seeds out and remove the skin.
2. Add the roasted jalapenos, tomato, onion, garlic, sugar, chile powder, pepper, and salt to a food processor or blender and pulse to combine. Add the cilantro, orange juice, lime juice, mirin, and beer and blend until smooth.
3. Transfer to a large, non-reactive bowl and submerge steak. Cover and refrigerate at least 4 hours, ideally overnight.
4. Grill steak over medium high heat, about 10 minutes for medium rare, or to your preference.
