# Cajun Chicken Alfredo

## Ingredients

- 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into strips
- 2 tbsp Cajun seasoning
- 1/4 tsp smoked paprika
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tbsp butter
- 6 gloves garlic, grated
- 1 c cream
- 1/2 c chicken broth
- 12 oz farfalle pasta, al dente
- 1 c parmesan cheese, grated

## Procedure

1. Season the chicken strips with the Cajun seasoning and smoked paprika, along with salt and pepper, ensuring that each piece is evenly coated.
2. In a large skillet over medium heat, add in the olive oil. Add in the chicken and cook until browned and cooked through. Set aside.
3. To the same pan, add the butter and garlic, sauteing until fragrant. Pour in the heavy cream and chicken broth and bring to a simmer, cooking until the sauce has thickened.
4. Stir in the grated parmesan. Once that's combined, add in the cooked pasta and 1/4 c pasta water, tossing to coat in the sauce. Return the chicken to pan and coat in sauce.
