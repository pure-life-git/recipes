# Bolognese (Bon Appetit)

## Ingredients

- 1 medium onion, diced
- 1 stalk celery, diced
- 1 large carrot, diced
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- 1 lb ground beef, 80/20
- 3 oz pancetta, finely chopped
- 1 c white wine
- 1/3 c tomato paste
- 1 bay leaf
- 1/4 tsp grated nutmeg
- 2 c chicken stock
- 1 c whole milk
- 1 lb pasta
- 2 oz parmesan, grated

## Procedure

1. In a large dutch oven over medium heat, add in the olive oil. Cook the ground beef, breaking into clumps, until brown. Remove from the pot.
2. Add in the pancetta and cook until the fat has rendered out and the pieces are crispy. Add in the onion, celery, and carrot and continue to cook until the vegetables have softened.
3. Add the ground beef back into the pot and then stir in the white wine, being sure to scrape the bottom as you do so. Simmer until the wine has reduced about half in volume.
4. Stir in the tomato paste, bay leaf, and nutmeg. Continue to cook until the tomato paste has darkened.
5. Pour in the stock and milk, along with a pinch of salt. Reduce heat to low, or place in a 325F oven with the lid on, and cook until the meat is tender, 2 1/2-3 hours.
6. Cook pasta to 2 minutes under al dente, then mix in to the sauce along with 1 cup pasta water and 1/3 cup grated parmesan and cook until the pasta is your preferred doneness.
