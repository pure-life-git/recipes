# Buffalo Chicken Dip

## Ingredients

- 20 oz canned chunk chicken, drained
- 3/4 c Frank's RedHot
- 16 oz cream cheese, softened
- 1 c ranch dressing
- 1 1/2 c cheddar cheese, shredded
- Celery, for dipping
- Crackers, for dipping

## Procedure

1. In a pot over medium heat, warm the chicken and RedHot. Stir in the cream cheese and ranch dressing and continue to cook until warm, 3-5 minutes.
2. Mix in 8 oz of cheese and transfer to a baking dish. Sprinkle remaining cheese on top.
3. Place into a 350F oven until dip is hot and bubbling, about 35 minutes.
4. Optionally, place under the broiler for 2 minutes, until the cheese on top is crispy.
5. Server with celery and crackers.
