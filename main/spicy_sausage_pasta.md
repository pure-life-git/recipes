# Spicy Sausage Pasta

## Ingredients

- 8oz rigatoni or penne pasta
- 2 tbsp butter
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1/2 lb spicy italian sausage
- 5 cloves garlic, grated
- 2 tbsp tomato paste
- 2 tsp dried basil
- 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
- 1 c white wine
- 1/2 c heavy cream
- 14 oz can diced plum tomatoes
- 1/2 c parmesan, grated

## Procedure

1. Cook the pasta according to the instructions for al dente on the box. While it's cooking, make the sauce.
2. In a large pan, heat the olive oil and butter over medium heat. Add the sausage to the pan, season with salt and pepper, and cook until browned.
3. Add the garlic, tomato paste, dried basil, and red pepper flakes. Cook until the tomato paste has darkened.
4. Pour in the wine, scraping the bottom of the pan as you do so. Bring to a simmer and cook off about half the wine.
5. Add in the heavy cream and tomatoes and bring back to a simmer. Cook until the sauce begins to thicken.
6. Pour in 1/4 cup of hot pasta water, along with the cooked pasta and parmesan. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, until the sauce has thickened.
