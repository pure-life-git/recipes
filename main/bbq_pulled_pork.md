# BBQ Pulled Pork

## Ingredients

- 4 lb boneless pork butt
- 3 tbsp brown sugar
- 1 tbsp salt
- 1 tbsp smoked paprika
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp ground cumin
- 1 tsp onion powder
- 2 tsp black pepper
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil
- 12 oz lager beer

## Procedure

1. Preheat oven to 300F. In a small bowl, combine brown sugar, salt, smoked paprika, garlic powder, ground cumin, onion powder, and black pepper.
2. Cut the pork into large pieces and rub with the spice mix. Let rest in the fridge 4-24 hours.
3. In a dutch oven over medium heat, add in the vegetable oil. Sear pork pieces on all sides.
4. Pour beer over the pork, cover, and cook in the 300F oven until the pork is fork tender, 3 hours. Uncover and continue to cook until the pork is easily pulled apart, 1-2 hours.
5. Shred the pork with two forks and mix with your preferred BBQ sauce.
