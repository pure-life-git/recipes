# Chicken Piccata

## Ingredients

- 4 boneless skinless chicken breasts, halved, pounded
- 2 tsp salt
- 2 tsp black pepper
- 1 c flour
- 6 tbsp butter, divided (3 tbsp + 3 tbsp, chilled, cubed)
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- 1 shallot, sliced
- 2 cloves garlic, grated
- 1/4 c white wine
- 1/2 c chicken stock
- 4 tsp capers, drained
- 1 meyer lemon, zested, juiced
- Parsley, coarsely chopped

## Procedure

1. Season chicken breasts with salt and pepper and dredge in flour.
2. In a large skillet, heat 3 tbsp of butter and olive oil over medium-high heat. Fry chicken cutlets until golden brown on both sides, 3 minutes per side. Remove and set aside.
3. Add the shallot and garlic into the pan and cook until lightly caramelized.
4. Add in the stock and white wine and let simmer until reduced about half in volume.
5. Reduce heat to low and add capers, lemon juice, and lemon zest. Remove from heat and whisk in 3 tbsp chilled butter cubes.
6. Cover the chicken with sauce and garnish with parsley.
