# Simple Carne Asada

## Ingredients

- 3/4 c orange juice
- 1/2 c lemon juice
- 1/3 c lime juice
- 1 bunch fresh cilantro, chopped
- 1/2 c soy sauce
- 1 tbsp chili powder
- 1 tbsp ground cumin
- 1 tbsp ground paprika
- 1 tbsp ground black pepper
- 1 tsp finely chopped chipotles in adobo
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- 1/2 c olive oil
- 3 lb flank steak

## Procedure

1. Combine citrus juices, cilantro, soy sauce, chili powder, cumin, paprika, black pepper, chipotle, and oregano in a large glass or ceramic bowl.
2. Slowly whisk in olive oil until well combined. Set aside 1 cup of marinade
3. Pound steak to about 1/4 in thickness using a meat mallet. Poke all over with a fork and place in marinade. Cover and refrigerate for 1-24 hours.
4. Preheat a grill for medium high heat and lightly oil the grate. Pat steak dry and cook until desired doneness, about 5 minutes per side for medium rare.
5. Remove from heat and slice against the grain.
