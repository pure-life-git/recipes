# Mac & Chees-us

## Ingredients

- 1 lb cavatappi pasta
- 16 oz mozzarella, shredded
- 16 oz colby jack, shredded
- 8 oz mild cheddar, shredded
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 3 tbsp butter
- 3 tbsp flour
- 12 oz evaporated milk
- 2 c heavy cream (454g)
- 1 tbsp Dijon mustard

## Procedure

1. Preheat the oven to 350F. Cook the pasta to al dente according to the box and set aside.
2. Combine all 3 cheeses, mix together, and then divide evenly into 2 bowls. Do the same with the spices
3. In a large pot, melt the butter over medium heat. Mix in one half of the spice mixture and the flour until the mixture becomes a paste
4. Add in the evaporated milk, whisking until the mixture is thick. Add in the heavy cream and the rest of the seasoning, whisking until combined.
5. Whisk in the Dijon mustard and one half the cheese mixture. Add in the cheese slowly, waiting for each addition to melt before adding the next.
6. Stir in the pasta, making sure to coat it in the cheese sauce.
7. Add a layer of the pasta into a 9x13 baking dish, then layer with the shredded cheese mixture. Repeat this so that you finish with shredded cheese on top.
8. Bake at 350F for 25–30 minutes, until the cheese is melted and bubbly. Finish by broiling until the top is golden brown, about 2 minutes.
