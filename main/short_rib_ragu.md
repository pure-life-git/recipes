# Short Rib Ragu

## Ingredients

- 4 large garlic cloves, smashed
- 3 lb short ribs, english cut
- 2 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 yellow onion, diced
- 28 oz can peeled plum tomatoes
- 1 c red wine
- 1 c water
- 10 sprigs fresh oregano
- 1 tbsp fennel seeds
- 1 tbsp habanero hot sauce
- 1 tsp ground allspice
- 2 tbsp orange juice

## Procedure

1. Pat the short ribs dry and generously season on all sides with salt and pepper. Heat the olive oil in a large dutch oven and sear short ribs on all sides until golden brown. Remove and set aside.
2. Add in the onion and garlic cloves to the dutch oven and cook until the onion is translucent.
3. While the onion is cooking, crush tomatoes into roughly 1/2 inch pieces. Add into the dutch oven, along with red wine, water, oregano, fennel seeds, hot sauce and allspice, stirring to combine.
4. Put the short ribs back into the pot, cover with a lid, and transfer into a 325F oven and cook for 2 1/2-3 hours, until the short ribs are fork tender.
5. Remove and discard bones and oregano. Shred the short ribs with two forks and stir in the orange juice.
