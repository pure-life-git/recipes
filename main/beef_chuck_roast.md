# The Roast of Beef Chuck

## Ingredients

- 3 lb chuck roast
- 1 1/2 tsp salt
- 1 tsp black pepper
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 2 yellow onions, roughly chopped
- 6 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tbsp tomato paste
- 1 c red wine
- 2 c beef broth
- 2 tsp worcestershire sauce
- 4 sprigs fresh thyme
- 2 sprigs fresh rosemary
- 2 bay leaves
- 1 lb baby carrots
- 1 1/2 lb mini potatoes
- chopped parsley, for garnishing

## Procedure

1. Preheat oven to 300F. Season the roast with salt and pepper on all sides.
2. Heat olive oil in a dutch oven over medium heat. Sear all side of the roast until golden brown. Remove from pot and set aside.
3. Add the onions to the pot and cook until beginning to caramelize. Toss in the garlic and cook till fragrant.
4. Pour in the red wine, making sure to scrape the bottom of the pan as you do so. Then add broth, worcestershire, and tomato paste. Bundle the thyme, rosemary, and bay leaves with some twine and toss them in as well.
5. Return the meat to the pot and cover with a lid. Transfer to the oven and cook at 300F for 1 1/2 hours. Remove the lid, add the potatoes and carrots, and continue to cook for an additional 2-2 1/2 hours, until the meat is fork tender.
