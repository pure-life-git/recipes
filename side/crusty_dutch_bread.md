# Crusty Dutch Bread

## Ingredients

- 2 1/4 tsp active dry yeast
- 1 tsp sugar
- 1 1/4 c warm water (300g)
- 1 1/4 tsp salt
- 3 1/3 c flour (400g)

## Procedure

1. Gently mix the yeast, sugar, and warm water in a large bowl and let sit for 5-10 minutes, until foaming.
2. Add flour to the bowl. Mix until barely combined, then add salt and continue mixing until everything is incorporated.
3. Cover with a towel and let rise in a warm spot until doubled in size, about an hour.
4. Turn dough out onto a lightly floured work surfaced and shape into a round loaf by folding the corners into the middle until it becomes tighter and resists your folds.
5. Flour a proofing basket or large bowl and place dough in seam side down. Cover with a towel and let rise another 30 minutes.
6. While the dough is proofing, preheat the oven to 460F with a lidded dutch oven inside/
7. Place the dough on a piece of parchment paper and score the top of it. Place it carefully into the preheated dutch oven, along with 1-2 ice cubes, and cover. Bake for 30 minutes. Remove the lid and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, until a brown crust has formed.
8. Remove the bread from the dutch oven and allow to cool completely before slicing.
