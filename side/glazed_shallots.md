# Glazed Shallots

## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 lb small shallots, peeled, halved
- 1/2 c white wine
- 1 c chicken stock
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 1 tsp salt
- 3 tbsp butter

## Procedure

1. Place the shallots in a single layer at the bottom of a large skillet. Add the wine, chicken stock, sugar, salt, and 2 tbsp butter and bring to a boil.
2. Lower the heat to a simmer and continue to cook until the shallots are fork tender, about 15 minutes.
3. Raise heat to medium-high and toss the shallots until they begin to brown and the sauce has thickened. Remove from heat and add 1 tbsp of butter before serving.
