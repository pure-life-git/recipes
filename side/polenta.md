# Polenta

## Ingredients

- 1 c fine cornmeal
- 4 tbsp butter
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp black pepper

## Procedure

1. Bring 4 cups of water to a boil over high heat. Add in 1 tsp salt and cornmeal, whisking until the mixture thickens
2. Reduce heat to low and cook for at least 45 minutes, stirring every 10-15 minutes, until polenta is swollen and tastes cooked. Thin with up to 1 cup of water if needed
3. Add butter to the pot and stir until combined. Serve immediately
