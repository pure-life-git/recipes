# Spanish Rice

## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 c long grain white rice
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 2 tbsp butter
- 1 garlic clove, minced
- 1 tsp chili powder
- 1/2 tsp cumin
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 2 c broth
- 1 1/4 c canned tomato sauce
- 3 tbsp cilantro, chopped

## Procedure

1. Rinse rice until no longer cloudy.

### Instant Pot

2. Using the "Saute" function, add in the butter, olive oil, rice, and garlic, stirring for 3 minutes.
3. Turn the Instant Pot off and add in the chili powder, cumin, salt, broth, and tomato sauce. Stir to combine, ensuring the rice is completely covered.
4. Cover the Instant Pot and pressure cook for 4 minutes with a natural release of 10 minutes. Remove lid, add in the cilantro, and fluff with a fork.

### Stove Top

2. In a large pot over medium heat, add in the butter and oil. Once the butter has melted, add in the rice and garlic, sauteing for a few minutes.
3. Add in the chili powder, cumin, salt, broth, and tomato sauce, stirring to combine. Increase the heat to high and bring to a boil, before reducing to a simmer.
4. Place a lid on the pot and continue to simmer for 12-14 minutes, until the rice has absorbed all the liquid.
5. Add in the cilantro and fluff with a fork.
