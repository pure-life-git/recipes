# Cornbread

## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 c cornmeal
- 1 1/2 c flour
- 3/4 c sugar
- 1 tbsp baking powder
- 1 1/2 tsp salt
- 3/4 tsp baking soda
- 1 1/8 c sour cream
- 3/4 c buttermilk
- 2 large eggs
- 3 tbsp butter, melted + 2 tbsp butter
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil

## Procedure

1. Preheat the oven to 425F with a 12" cast iron skillet inside.
2. In a medium bowl, mix together the cornmeal, flour, sugar, baking powder, salt, and baking soda.
3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the sour cream, buttermilk, eggs, butter, and vegetable oil.
4. Combine the wet and dry ingredients and whisk until combined. Remove skillet from the oven, melt 2 tbsp of butter into it, and pour the batter in.
5. Return to oven and bake until a tester comes out clean, 20 minutes.
6. Let cool at least 5 minutes before serving.
